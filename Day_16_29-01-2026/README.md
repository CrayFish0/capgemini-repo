# Day 16 – 29.01.2026: Communication and Soft Skills

## Topics Covered
- Effective Communication in the workplace
- Active listening techniques
- Professional email writing
- Body language and non-verbal communication
- Interview preparation tips (verbal communication)

## Email Writing Template
```
Subject: Clear and concise subject line

Dear [Name],

Opening paragraph – state the purpose clearly.

Body – provide details, keeping it concise.

Closing – next steps / request / thanks.

Regards,
[Your Name]
[Designation]
```

## Notes
- Speak clearly; avoid filler words (um, uh, like).
- STAR format for interview answers: Situation, Task, Action, Result.
- Dress professionally; maintain eye contact during interviews.
