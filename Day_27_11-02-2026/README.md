## Topics Covered
- Business English communication

## Professional Communication Tips
1. **Clarity** – Use simple, direct language.
2. **Conciseness** – Say more with fewer words.
3. **Tone** – Stay professional and respectful.
4. **Grammar** – Subject-verb agreement, tense consistency.

## Common Email Phrases
| Situation          | Phrase                                       |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Opening            | "I hope this email finds you well."          |
| Requesting         | "Could you please provide..."                |
| Clarifying         | "To clarify / As per our discussion..."      |
| Closing            | "Please let me know if you need anything."   |
| Apologizing        | "I apologize for the inconvenience."         |

## GD (Group Discussion) Do's and Don'ts
- **Do:** Listen actively, support with facts, be respectful.
- **Don't:** Interrupt, speak too fast, dominate the discussion.
